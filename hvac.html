  • Client Calls

    Client Calls within a Service CRM are a crucial feature designed to streamline and manage incoming and outgoing communication between your business and clients. This feature ensures that every client interaction is tracked, managed, and optimized for effective service delivery. Here’s how the Client Calls feature functions and benefits your business:

    Call Logging and Tracking
    Automatic Logging:

    The CRM automatically logs details of each incoming and outgoing call, including date, time, duration, and the client’s contact information. This ensures that all interactions are recorded without manual entry.

    Call History:

    Users can access a comprehensive call history for each client, allowing them to review past communications and maintain continuity in service.
